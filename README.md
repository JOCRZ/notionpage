## Navigating the Chapters
Click on any **chapter number** to instantly open and read that specific chapter.



## Find a Chapter Quickly
Use the **Find** button to search for a particular chapter by **number** or **keyword**.



## Explore by Arc
Each **Arc** in the left panel represents a major story segment. 
Click on an **Arc name** to view all chapters or playlists within that arc.


## Arc Playlists
Select an **Arc Playlist** to read or watch related content in sequence.
